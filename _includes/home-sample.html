PLCMOS Evaluation

To ensure the highest quality of service, aoip.ai incorporates DNSMOS evaluation, a key metric in the DNS Challenge. This psychoacoustic evaluation method quantifies the denoising success of audio streams, allowing software engineers to assess and enhance the clarity and intelligibility of voice communication.

eGeMAPS Evaluation

Aoip.ai's robust testing framework includes PLCMOS evaluation, a fundamental aspect of the PLC Challenge. This evaluation focuses on the perceptual quality of packet loss concealment, crucial in maintaining high-quality audio transmission. It provides engineers with insights into the resilience and reliability of VoIP communications under varying network conditions.
